About Me

Highly curious individual with proven track record of creativity, problem solving, and in-depth analytical thinking. I specialize in working with mainly statistical languages and packages, specifically Python, R, SQL, and Map Reduce. I use these tools and work with business leaders to solve complex business problems that require an "outside-of-the-box" approach. A few of my projects (listed below) have included: business dashboard (R/Shiny), hourly/predictive metrics (Python), and regression-based slotting logic (Hive/Python). There are three main values that drive how I approach work and analysis:

  • 1. Simplicity. This is at the heart of what I do - keeping code clean, creating intuitive reporting, centralizing information when possible.
  • 2. Brevity. The less I'm able to communicate, the more I will think about what is truly important to the audience.
  • 3. Integration. How is this report/data table going to affect my peers? Can it be used to benefit another department? If so, how do I help integrate this information so that there are not two databases consuming resources for the same output? I'm a believer that if you do it right the first time, you will save a signifcant amount of resources in the long-run.
With these values at the heart of what I do, my goal is to end each day knowing that I provided value to my end-user (customer). I strive to improve every day and am an avid reader of new languages and better methods to achieve the end goal. When not at work, I enjoy hiking, running, rock climbing, and all things nature.
